## Executive Summary

Conducted comprehensive review of all public-facing documentation for the MORE Club to ensure appropriate balance between encouraging community participation and setting realistic expectations. The club is in **founding phase** but documentation presented it as an established organization with active programming.

### Key Finding

**Primary Issue:** Documentation created expectations for active, established club operations (weekly rides, monthly meetings, membership benefits) when the organization is actually in early founding/planning stages.

**Risk:** New members could be disappointed or frustrated by gap between promises and reality, damaging trust and reputation.

**Solution:** Comprehensive transparency about founding phase status while maintaining enthusiasm and invitation for founding members to help build the community.
